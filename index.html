About

I write for people, not just platforms.

I am a Content and Communications Specialist based in Bengaluru, specializing in building narratives for B2B and consumer brands across Fintech, Enterprise Tech, Sports, and Media. I'm a graduate of Christ University with a BA in Journalism (Honours). I approach storytelling with a reporter's eye for detail and a strategist's focus on impact.

My most recent role was at Zensciences Business Solutions, where I led end-to-end communication for global enterprise clients. My toolkit ranges from high-level thought leadership and brand books to sharp paid copy and persuasive pitch narratives. I bridge the gap between complex technical solutions and compelling human stories.

Outside of work, I am usually deep in football analysis or exploring cinema of all shapes and sizes. I also enjoy spending time in the kitchen, making dishes that make me feel like a Michelin-star chef, and hitting the gym to work on my fitness and self-esteem.
